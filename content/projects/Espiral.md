{{< img src="images/espiral.png" alt="Espiral cover image" >}}

Espiral is a game I made in Twine for the [Last 3 Minutes Jam](https://itch.io/jam/last-3-minutes-jam) about a anxious person having to face their interview in the next three minutes.

It has 3 endings, but just 2 are actually very different. The main theme is anxiety and low self-esteem, and how that triggers overthinking to the point of panic and giving up.
