Espiral

July 7, 2026

Espiral means “spiral” in Portuguese.

Espiral is a game I made in Twine for the Last 3 Minutes Jam about a anxious person having to face their interview in the next three minutes.

It has 3 endings, but just 2 are actually very different. The main theme is anxiety and low self-esteem, and how that triggers overthinking to the point of panic and giving up.

Play it on itch.io!
